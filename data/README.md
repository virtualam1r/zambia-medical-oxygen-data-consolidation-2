# Public data files

These files are cleaned and structured research outputs derived from public reports and webpages. They are not copies of original reports or a raw-source archive.

## Core data

- `core_metrics_by_year.csv`: 18 grouped metrics with 2020–2026 values, evidence codes, and a 2026 strategy-goal column from the 2022 plan.
- `strategy_baseline_target_comparison.csv`: 14 strategy indicators with baselines, targets, latest public data, evidence dates, comparison status, and comparability notes.
- `master_observation_dataset.csv`: all 2,174 normalized observations with scope, evidence status, source reference, notes, and quality flags.
- `psa_site_reconciliation.csv`: 27 publicly identifiable facility sites with programme overlap and counting cautions.

## Supporting data

- `dated_event_log.csv`: 35 selected post-2020 events with evidence period, scope, source, and limitations.
- `status_definitions.csv`: definitions for the evidence classifications used in the master dataset.

Original public sources are listed in `../sources/sources.csv`. No source PDFs or full-text extractions are included.
