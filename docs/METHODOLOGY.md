# Methodology

## Research question

What can public evidence establish about changes in Zambia's medical oxygen system, particularly since 2020 and the National Medical Oxygen Strategic Plan 2022–2026?

## Source hierarchy

Evidence was prioritized in this order:

1. Zambia Ministry of Health strategies and official records.
2. Direct facility assessments and implementation evaluations.
3. UN, World Bank, Global Fund, UNICEF, WHO, UNOPS, PATH, Unitaid, and other implementing-partner records.
4. Peer-reviewed studies.
5. Named institutional or technical-official statements.
6. News reports quoting identifiable officials.

Lower-strength sources are retained only when their status and limitations are explicit.

## Normalization

Each observation records:

- Evidence period and reference year.
- Geography and scope.
- Domain and metric.
- Numeric value and source wording.
- Unit and denominator.
- Evidence status.
- Source reference and URL when available.
- Notes and quality flags.

## Classification rules

- Actual: observed, delivered, installed, commissioned, or reported operating at the stated time and scope.
- Baseline: strategy starting value.
- Estimate: approximate or bounded value.
- Projection: expected future value.
- Target: planned future result.
- Budget: planned, committed, or reported financial value.
- Capacity: designed capability, not measured output.
- Derived: transparent calculation or source reconciliation.
- Context: relevant background or claim-quality record.

## Comparability

National inventories, programme outputs, and samples of 8, 11, 53, 91, or 116 hospitals are not merged into false national trends. Empty cells mean no comparable public figure was found; they do not mean zero.

## Link verification

Published source links were checked automatically on 17 July 2026. Some sites block automated requests while remaining accessible in a browser; these are marked `access-restricted`, not broken. Links returning 404 or 410 are marked `unavailable` for review.

## Exclusions

The public repository excludes source PDFs, full-text extractions, and unrelated project files.
