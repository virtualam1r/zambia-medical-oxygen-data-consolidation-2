# Data limitations

- The 2020 assessment covered eight hospitals; the late-2023 BMES covered 91 hospitals. Neither is a full national census.
- Installed or commissioned equipment is not automatically functional, producing at capacity, or supplying patients.
- Design capacity is not actual production. Cylinder water capacity must not be treated as free-gas volume without pressure and reference conditions.
- Programme reach and intended catchment populations are not verified beneficiaries.
- Several new units were installed at facilities with pre-existing plants. New assets do not always represent new oxygen-producing sites.
- The public record contains denominator and wording conflicts. These are preserved rather than silently resolved.
- The Global Fund's broader US$362 million 2024–2026 grants and more than US$144 million in Zambia C19RM funding include oxygen but are not oxygen-only budgets.
- The reported 8,000- and 10,400-cylinder procurements lack accessible Global Fund primary corroboration and must not be combined.
- Current national plant functionality, cylinder inventory, oxygen purity, downtime, repair time, utilization, and patient outcomes remain unavailable publicly.
- Link availability can change after the stated verification date.
