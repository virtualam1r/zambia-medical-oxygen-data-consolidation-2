# Selected consolidated values

This file lists selected values from the structured datasets. Status and scope are included so that unlike measures are not combined.

## Production infrastructure

- 2020 actual, 8-hospital assessment: 5 PSA plants installed; 4 functional.
- 2022 national-plan baseline: 10 public PSA plants installed; 9 functional.
- 2024 actual, World Bank programme: 15 of 15 mobile PSA plants installed and commissioned at named hospitals.
- April 2026 actual, Global Fund–UNOPS programme: 6 of 6 planned plants commissioned.
- April 2026 derived minimum: at least 27 distinct facility sites publicly identified with an installed or commissioned PSA plant. Current functionality was not established.

## Production reliability

- Late-2023 actual, assessed PSA plants in the 91-hospital survey: 73% operated year-round.
- Late-2023 actual, same assessment: 64% operated 24 hours per day.

## Bulk supply and distribution

- 2022 national-plan baseline: 0 public facilities with bulk liquid oxygen.
- December 2024 derived minimum: at least 2 publicly identifiable hospital LOX installations.
- Late-2023 actual, 91-hospital assessment: 66 facilities lacked a bulk oxygen source.
- Late-2023 actual, same assessment: 21 facilities had medical-gas piping.
- 2022 actual, WHO programme: 4,000 pre-filled cylinders supplied; reported value US$744,000.
- 2023 reported claim: 8,000 cylinders procured. No accessible primary ZAMMSA or Global Fund record corroborated the count.

## Workforce and maintenance

- 2023 actual, national training programme: 76 trainers completed the programme.
- By June 2024 actual, national training programme: 672 additional nurses trained.
- Late-2023 actual, 91-hospital assessment: 42 facilities had no available biomedical engineer or technician.
- December 2025 actual: 2 colleges were using the oxygen-management and maintenance curriculum.

## Patient access and monitoring

- Late-2023 actual, 91-hospital assessment: approximately 1 functional pulse oximeter per 10 beds.
- August 2024 actual, 116 responding hospitals: 37.96% reported reliably available perioperative oxygen.
- 2026 national-plan target: 60% of public hospitals with strengthened oxygen supply systems.
- Public national actual for the 60% indicator: not identified as of 17 July 2026.
